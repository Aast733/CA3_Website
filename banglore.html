Bangalore

Silicon Valley of India

Bangalore, now known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of Karnataka, India. Often referred to as the "Silicon Valley of India," it’s a major tech hub, housing numerous multinational companies and startups. The city is known for its pleasant, moderate climate throughout the year, which is rare in many parts of India. Bangalore boasts a unique blend of cultures, with people from all over the country, making it a melting pot of traditions and cuisines. Its food scene ranges from local South Indian flavors to international cuisine, and it’s famous for cafes, restaurants, and street food.

Restaurants

Flavourful Journey

The Shao

Cuisine Focus: Shao primarily serves Chinese and Asian-inspired dishes, with a focus on authentic flavors crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Specialties: The restaurant is known for its dim sum varieties, Peking duck, and flavorful seafood dishes, including lobster and prawns prepared with regional twists.

Rim naam

Rim Naam is an enchanting, open-air restaurant nestled within the lush gardens of The Oberoi in Bangalore, known for its serene ambiance and scenic location. Positioned on an island-like setting surrounded by a beautiful lily pond, it creates a magical dining experience under the canopy of trees and softly lit lanterns. The restaurant specializes in Thai cuisine, presenting authentic flavors crafted from the freshest ingredients and aromatic herbs. Guests enjoy a diverse menu featuring dishes like Som Tum (green papaya salad), Pad Thai noodles, and a range of curries. The ambiance feels both luxurious and close to nature, perfect for romantic dinners or special gatherings. Soft Thai music and the gentle sound of flowing water add to the calming atmosphere.

Onlu Palace

The interiors are charming, with rustic decor, wooden furniture, and a cozy, homely ambiance. An open-air garden setting adds a serene, natural feel, enhancing the dining experience. Meen Eleittad, a delicately spiced fish grilled in a banana leaf, is one of their signature dishes. Tiger prawns marinated in red chili paste and Mangalorean chicken curry are also must-try specialties. They use fresh, authentic ingredients like tamarind, coconut, and local spices, adding depth to each dish.

Mikusu

Mikusu is a sophisticated Asian restaurant at Conrad Bengaluru in Ulsoor, Bangalore, known for its refined blend of Japanese, Chinese, and Thai cuisines. It combines authentic Asian flavors with a contemporary touch, emphasizing high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. Guests enjoy a luxurious ambiance, with decor that pays homage to East Asian aesthetics, and the restaurant offers an array of crafted Asian cocktails and curated sake to complement the dishes.
