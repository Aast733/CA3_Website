Delhi

City of rallies

Delhi, the vibrant capital of India, is a city rich in history, culture, and diversity. Located in northern India, it sprawls along the banks of the Yamuna River and serves as both a major political center and a cultural hub. Delhi is home to iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate, which stand as reminders of its ancient past, colonial history, and modern aspirations. The city offers a blend of the old and the new, with bustling markets in Old Delhi contrasting sharply with the sleek skyscrapers and commercial centers of New Delhi.

Restaurants

Flavourful Journey

Baukhara

Bukhara in Delhi is a renowned, award-winning restaurant celebrated for its authentic North Indian cuisine, especially its tandoori and kebab dishes. Located at ITC Maurya, it has consistently maintained its reputation as one of the best fine dining experiences in India. Known for its rustic ambiance, Bukhara features wooden furniture, earthy tones, and an open kitchen that adds to its charm.

Dum pukht

Dum Pukht in Delhi is a renowned fine-dining restaurant celebrated for its authentic Awadhi cuisine and regal ambiance. Located within ITC Maurya, it brings to life the legendary slow-cooking techniques and rich flavors of North Indian cuisine. The term "Dum Pukht" refers to a method where food is cooked on a slow flame, often sealed in dough to lock in the flavors and aromas. Signature dishes include the Dum Pukht Biryani and Murgh Khushk Purdah, delicacies that highlight the elegance and subtlety of Awadhi spices.

Karavalli

Karavalli is a renowned South Indian restaurant located in Delhi, known for its authentic coastal flavors and traditional recipes. Specializing in the culinary heritage of the western coastal regions of India, Karavalli draws from Karnataka, Goa, and Kerala influences to create a unique dining experience. The restaurant is celebrated for its use of fresh spices, coconut, and local ingredients, all key elements in coastal cuisine.

Indian accent

Restaurants in Delhi embody a lively, vibrant culture that perfectly mirrors the city’s energetic pulse. With its rich tapestry of diverse cuisines, dining in Delhi offers a unique mix of Indian authenticity and contemporary flair. You’ll find everything from local dhabas (small eateries) serving up spicy street food to high-end establishments offering exquisite takes on North Indian classics. Many restaurants in Delhi aim to create an immersive experience, where the ambiance matches the food's authenticity. At traditional spots, you'll hear upbeat Bollywood tunes and conversations
